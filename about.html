Our Story

Our story began with a simple love for cycling. Joe The Goat, our founder, started this store with a vision to create a community hub where cyclists could find not only the best bikes but also a place to connect, share experiences, and get expert advice.
Over the years, our dedication to customer satisfaction and our deep knowledge of the industry have helped us grow into the trusted bike store we are today.

As we look to the future, our goal remains the same: to be the go-to destination for cyclists in our area. We are constantly seeking new ways to enhance our offerings and better serve our customers. Whether you're buying your first bike, upgrading to a high-performance model, or just looking for some friendly advice, we're here to help.

Nestled in the heart of Queenstown, our bike store stands as the ultimate destination for all cycling enthusiasts. Whether you’re a local or just passing through, we offer a comprehensive range of high-quality bikes and accessories designed to meet your every need. Our store is situated in a prime spot, offering easy access to Queenstown's bustling town center and the breathtaking natural landscapes that surround it. Whether you’re exploring the town’s lively cafes, boutiques, and restaurants or heading out to experience the renowned outdoor activities Queenstown is famous for, our location ensures that you're never far from top-notch cycling gear and expert advice.

Being at the heart of Queenstown means we’re deeply connected to the local cycling community. Our knowledgeable staff are not only experts in biking but also passionate about Queenstown’s outdoor scene. We’re here to share insider tips on the best trails, scenic routes, and local cycling events, helping you make the most of your time in this extraordinary area.

Our Location
